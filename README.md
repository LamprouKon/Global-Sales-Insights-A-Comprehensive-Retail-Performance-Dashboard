# 📊 Global Sales Insights  
### A Comprehensive Retail Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

**Global Sales Insights** is a data-driven Power BI dashboard designed to provide a holistic view of retail market performance across global regions, product categories, and customer segments.

Built on the Global Superstore dataset, this dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The primary goal of this project is to:

- Enable data-driven decision-making
- Identify sales growth patterns and performance drivers
- Understand customer behavior and segmentation
- Support strategic and operational retail optimization

---

## 🔎 Dashboard Sections

### 📈 Sales Performance
- Yearly and quarterly sales tracking
- Monthly performance trends
- Category and sub-category breakdown
- Top-performing products analysis
- Geographic sales distribution

### 👥 Customer Analysis
- Total orders trend
- Regional and market-level customer distribution
- Customer segmentation (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Top customers by revenue and order volume

---


## 🎯 Business Value

This dashboard enables business leaders and sales strategists to:

- Detect high-growth markets
- Identify underperforming segments
- Optimize inventory allocation
- Align marketing focus with revenue drivers
- Monitor customer acquisition and retention trends

---

## 📊 Dataset

Global Superstore Retail Dataset  
(Used for analytical and educational purposes)

The project demonstrates how structured data modeling and interactive BI design can convert complex retail data into clear, strategic insights.
