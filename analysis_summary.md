# 📊 Global Sales Insights – Analytical Summary

## 📌 Project Context

This Power BI dashboard analyzes global retail sales performance across products, regions, and customer segments.  

The analysis is structured to support executive-level decision-making through interactive visualization and KPI tracking.

---

# 1️⃣ Sales Performance Analysis

<img width="610" height="342" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9d71e408-6504-4571-90e2-11ee5e719121" />


### 🔎 Key Insights

- Strong year-over-year growth observed (26% increase vs previous year).
- Q4 appears to be the strongest performing quarter.
- Monthly breakdown reveals seasonal spikes in late Q3 and Q4.
- Technology category dominates overall revenue contribution.
- Specific sub-categories (e.g., Phones, Copiers) drive disproportionate value.

### 💡 Business Implication

Focus marketing spend and inventory planning on Q3–Q4 cycles and high-performing sub-categories to maximize ROI.
